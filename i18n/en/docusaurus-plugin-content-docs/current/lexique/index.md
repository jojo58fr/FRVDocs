## Alphabetical VTubing Glossary

<div class="lexicon-wiki">
  <p class="lexicon-summary">This directory gathers every important term in individual entries. Each alphabetical section groups definitions and links to their dedicated pages.</p>

  <section class="lexicon-section">
    <div class="lexicon-letter-row">
      <span class="lexicon-letter-heading">#</span>
      <span class="lexicon-letter-divider"></span>
    </div>
    <div class="lexicon-grid">
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/2-5d-idol">
        <div>
          <strong>2.5D Idol</strong>
          <p>A VTuber idol performing in both virtual and live-action settings, sometimes without revealing their face.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="lexicon-section">
    <div class="lexicon-letter-row">
      <span class="lexicon-letter-heading">A</span>
      <span class="lexicon-letter-divider"></span>
    </div>
    <div class="lexicon-grid">
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/asmrtist">
        <div>
          <strong>ASMRtist</strong>
          <p>A VTuber specializing in ASMR: soft triggers (sleep, ear cleaning) or more suggestive ones; also called an ASMRist.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/avtuber">
        <div>
          <strong>AVTuber</strong>
          <p>A VTuber producing R-18 content outside platforms like YouTube/Twitch, sometimes tied to niche agencies.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/affiliate">
        <div>
          <strong>Affiliate</strong>
          <p>On Twitch, an Affiliate is a streamer eligible for monetization; in Hololive, it refers to inactive talents who may still appear or sell merchandise.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/agence-vtuber">
        <div>
          <strong>VTuber Agency</strong>
          <p>A VTuber agency recruits, trains, and manages avatar-based creators, handling tech, promotion, monetization, and event organization.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/akasupa">
        <div>
          <strong>Akasupa</strong>
          <p>A high-value YouTube donation, recognizable by its red highlight (akai), essentially a premium Super Chat.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/anti">
        <div>
          <strong>Anti</strong>
          <p>A troll or hater openly criticizing a VTuber.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/avatar-virtuel">
        <div>
          <strong>Virtual Avatar</strong>
          <p>A visual and identity representation of a person online; for VTubers, it conveys their personality, emotions, and narrative universe.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="lexicon-section">
    <div class="lexicon-letter-row">
      <span class="lexicon-letter-heading">B</span>
      <span class="lexicon-letter-divider"></span>
    </div>
    <div class="lexicon-grid">
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/babiniku">
        <div>
          <strong>Babiniku</strong>
          <p>A male VTuber embodying a female character while openly acknowledging his real gender.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/black-company">
        <div>
          <strong>Black company</strong>
          <p>An exploitative agency mistreating its talents (non-payment, doxxing, harassment).</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/bj">
        <div>
          <strong>Broadcast Jockey</strong>
          <p>Korean term for a livestreamer.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="lexicon-section">
    <div class="lexicon-letter-row">
      <span class="lexicon-letter-heading">C</span>
      <span class="lexicon-letter-divider"></span>
    </div>
    <div class="lexicon-grid">
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/capture-mouvement">
        <div>
          <strong>Motion Capture</strong>
          <p>Motion capture records body positions using sensors to animate an avatar by applying the data to its digital skeleton.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/carrier-pigeon">
        <div>
          <strong>Carrier pigeon</strong>
          <p>A viewer relaying messages from one stream to another, often perceived as stream sniping.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/chroma-key">
        <div>
          <strong>Chroma key</strong>
          <p>Filming a subject against a solid background to isolate and overlay them onto a digital environment, used for stylized overlays in VTubing/streaming.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/clipper">
        <div>
          <strong>Clipper</strong>
          <p>A channel making clips from streams, either official or fan-made.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/clipping">
        <div>
          <strong>Clipping</strong>
          <p>Creating videos from stream highlights to immortalize key moments.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/collab">
        <div>
          <strong>Collab</strong>
          <p>A collaboration featuring multiple VTubers in a shared stream.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="lexicon-section">
    <div class="lexicon-letter-row">
      <span class="lexicon-letter-heading">D</span>
      <span class="lexicon-letter-divider"></span>
    </div>
    <div class="lexicon-grid">
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/delivery">
        <div>
          <strong>Delivery (haishin)</strong>
          <p>Translation of 配信: the Japanese word for "stream" or "broadcast."</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/diffusion-en-direct">
        <div>
          <strong>Livestream</strong>
          <p>A real-time broadcast allowing immediate interaction with viewers via chat and reactions.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/doxxing">
        <div>
          <strong>Doxxing</strong>
          <p>Revealing a talent's real identity: a condemned act that breaks anonymity.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/dullahan">
        <div>
          <strong>Dullahan</strong>
          <p>Korean term for a faceless streamer, often using static images.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/debut-stream">
        <div>
          <strong>Debut Stream</strong>
          <p>The first official VTuber livestream, introducing their avatar, character, and launching their career.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="lexicon-section">
    <div class="lexicon-letter-row">
      <span class="lexicon-letter-heading">E</span>
      <span class="lexicon-letter-divider"></span>
    </div>
    <div class="lexicon-grid">
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/endurance">
        <div>
          <strong>Endurance Stream</strong>
          <p>A challenge stream focused on reaching a goal (subscribers, duration, achievements).</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/enjou">
        <div>
          <strong>Enjou</strong>
          <p>A massive online controversy; Japanese equivalent of "getting flamed."</p>
        </div>
      </a>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="lexicon-section">
    <div class="lexicon-letter-row">
      <span class="lexicon-letter-heading">F</span>
      <span class="lexicon-letter-divider"></span>
    </div>
    <div class="lexicon-grid">
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/fan-name">
        <div>
          <strong>Fan Name</strong>
          <p>The collective nickname given to a VTuber's fanbase.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/fanart">
        <div>
          <strong>Fanart</strong>
          <p>Creative works made by fans (drawings, animations, clips) depicting and promoting a VTuber.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/fandom">
        <div>
          <strong>Fandom</strong>
          <p>The VTuber's fan community, active across platforms producing memes, clips, and shared resources.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/fleshtuber">
        <div>
          <strong>Fleshtuber</strong>
          <p>A streamer who does not use a virtual model.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/free-chat">
        <div>
          <strong>Free Chat</strong>
          <p>A persistent chatroom created via a scheduled YouTube stream.</p>
        </div>
      </a>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="lexicon-section">
    <div class="lexicon-letter-row">
      <span class="lexicon-letter-heading">G</span>

    </div>

      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/gachikoi">

        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/graduation">

      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/guerrilla">

        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/gyakutotsu">
        <div>
          <strong>Gyakutotsu</strong>
          <p>Typically a birthday stream where the VTuber calls friends (reverse call-in).</p>
        </div>
      </a>
      <a class="lexicon-card" href="/docs/lexique/generation">
        <div>
          <strong>Generation (Gen)</strong>

        </div>
      </a>
    </div>
  </section>
