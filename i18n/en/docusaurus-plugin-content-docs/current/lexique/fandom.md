## Definition
A fandom is the collective group of fans surrounding a VTuber, often active on Discord servers, Twitter/X hashtags, Reddit, and blogs. They create memes, clips, and collaborative resources like wikis and playlists.

## Why it matters
- Encourages collaboration (translations, guides, remixes).  
- Helps measure popularity (followers, views, Super Chats).  
- Provides structure for moderation and community goals (concerts, charity drives).
