## Definition
In Japanese entertainment, an idol is a performer promoted for their image and personality. VTubing borrows this system by presenting talents as "virtual idols" active in streaming, singing, and dancing.

## Legacy for VTubing
- Idol groups and agencies inspired generation structures, fan culture, and merchandising.  
- VTubers embrace the format through concerts, singles, and close fanclub interactions.
